Our Company Culture

Our company culture can be described as open/informal. We welcome contributors' ideas and efforts to grow our brand.

We are committed to expanding our product offerings. We want to be your go-to store for easy, comfortable elegance.

As we grow, we want to maintain a predominantly U.S. made product line with perhaps a few items, which are clearly labeled, included to also make a difference for workers outside the U.S.
